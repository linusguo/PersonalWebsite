Yusheng is a graduate student pursuing his master's degree in computer science at Khoury College of Computer Sciences at Northeastern University. Before joining Khoury, Yusheng was a graduate researcher at Louisiana State University. His research focused on surface chemistry, material and colloidal science. Yusheng is a former software engineer intern at Aeyesafe and GourmetCarte. He is also a bass player in Spiral Dreams and Lychee, two local bands in Seattle who play rock, punk and R&B music.

Yusheng moved to the US from China since 2015. He is now living in Seattle.
